Automatic Solar Tracker Model

Build a solar tracker that follows light source using salvaged servo motors and micro controllers.

📋 Required Salvaged Parts:

  • 🔋 1x Salvaged 9V Battery or cell pack
  • ⚙️ 2x Micro Servo Motors
  • 📡 4x Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs)
  • 🔲 1x Mini solar panel

🚀 Step-by-Step Overview:

  1. Construct the pivoting frame using upcycled cardboard or wood strips.
  2. Mount one servo at the base for horizontal rotation, and the second on top for vertical pivot.
  3. Place LDR sensors at the 4 corners of the solar panel, separated by small cardboard walls.
  4. Connect all components to your microcontroller board and upload the tracker script.
Beginner

Upcycled Mini Wind Turbine

Generate electricity from simple air flow using an old DC motor and plastic bottles.

📋 Required Salvaged Parts:

  • ⚙️ 1x Salvaged high-RPM DC motor (from old CD drive or toy)
  • 🔴 1x LED Light (Red/Green)
  • 🥤 Plastic bottle (for cutting blades)
  • 📏 Cardboard or plastic tube for support stand

🚀 Step-by-Step Overview:

  1. Carefully cut fan blades from the curved surface of a plastic drink bottle.
  2. Attach the blade center hub securely onto the shaft of your salvage DC motor.
  3. Solder the motor terminal wires directly to the LED legs (check polarity).
  4. Blow air onto the blades to spin the motor. Watch the LED glow green!

🌱 Why Upcycling E-Waste Matters

Electronic waste contains high-value microcontrollers, copper wiring, gear assemblies, lithium battery cells, and sensors. By reclaiming these components instead of dumping them, we prevent toxic heavy metals from contaminating landfills while offering low-cost educational resources to innovators.

90% Less Waste
